# Practice Crops and Plant Grouping

## Why this note exists

Use short-cycle practice crops to debug lighting, airflow, and watering before relying on the system for a more sensitive grow.

## Best early crops

- Microgreens for fastest feedback
- Lettuce for quick repeatable greens
- Basil for pruning and canopy practice
- Radishes for short root-crop cycles
- Peppers as the next step once the environment is stable

## Suggested learning path

1. Weeks 1-2: microgreens and lettuce
2. Weeks 2-4: basil and radishes
3. Weeks 4-8: peppers

## Grouping guidance

### Works well together

- Lettuce, basil, and microgreens

### Sometimes okay

- Peppers and basil

### Poor fit in the same run

- Peppers with lettuce because peppers want more height and warmth
- Radishes mixed into a general shared tray setup because their space and harvest cycle are different
